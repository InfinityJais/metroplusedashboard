üéØ Business Problem

Traditional investment decisions rely on static reports and broker opinions, leading to mispriced assets and poor yield-vs-growth trade-offs. MetroPulse systematically identifies micro-markets that offer the best balance between rental income and capital appreciation.

Project Demo & Walkthrough

Step-by-Step Implementation

01

Synthetic Data & Cleaning

Generated realistic economic datasets using noise-injected time-series formulas to simulate gradual price appreciation and infrastructure-driven growth. Cleaned and validated data using Python to ensure high integrity.

# Example: Noise Injection
Value(t) = Base_Market_Value + f(Time) + Noise
02

Feature Engineering

Converted raw prices into investment-relevant features like Rental Yield %, YoY Growth, and Liquidity Scores.

03

Investment Scoring & Clustering

Developed an Investment Scoring Framework combining rule-based logic with HDBSCAN ML Clustering. This segment identifies "Balanced," "Yield-Focused," and "Speculative" markets.

04

Backtesting & Validation

Validated the model by measuring if high investment scores at time t accurately predicted price appreciation at t + 12 months.
